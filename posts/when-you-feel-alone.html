When You Feel Alone

May 2026

You know when you feel you’re alone?

You know when you feel so alone that you don’t even feel you are with someone when you are with someone?

You don’t even like yourself when you are with that someone.

And you feel alone.

We got a moment of individuality for ourselves.

And then we didn’t want to go back.
