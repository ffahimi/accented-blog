Short Letter to a Barista

May 2026

This is my letter to a barista.

I think most times I think about writing to someone like you— the ideal barista.

There are several ideal barista archetypes for me. All of them have one thing in common.

They give you coffee.

They might or might not make your day with a smile. But they always teach you something.

And no matter what age they are or who you are, they treat you with dignity.

That’s a great thing about great baristas.

They are there because they want to be free. They would rather sacrifice an office job to be there.

And they do their work perfectly, because they feel exactly what is on the other side.
