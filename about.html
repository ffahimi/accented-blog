About

Accented is a simple blog for essays.

The goal is not to publish often. The goal is to publish things worth returning to: thoughts on work, building, research, technology, markets, independence, and the long process of becoming more precise.

This site is intentionally minimal. No feed of distractions, no heavy design, just text.
